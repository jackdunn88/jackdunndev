about me

Welcome To My Website! I am currently working on several programming languages and projects while exploring the consumer technology journalism sector with one of my brands, JD Tekk. My aim is to create services that can change and influence lives built from the ground up with love. 

I hope you are able to learn something from my website,
Jack Dunn

brands

Since June 2019, I established the technology journalism brand JD Tekk aimed to provide users with a one stop shop to non bias technology news. JD Tekk has grew faster than my expectations and is something that I will be continuing to develop in 2020. The second brand I am beginning to work on is Apollo Apps. A software and web development company aimed to change lives and influence others into the world of coding. My mission with the Apollo brand will begin during 2020.

programming

I am  interested in the development of software and applications and I am beginning to learn Swift, a programming language used for iOS App Development. I am also expanding my knowledge of HTML, CSS And Javascript within the bootstrap framework to continue my work in Web Development. 

My main programming language I am currently learning is Python.
